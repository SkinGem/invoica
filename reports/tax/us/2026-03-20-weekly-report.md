### 2. Digital Services Tax at Federal and State Levels
- **Jurisdiction**: Federal
- **What Changed/Proposed**: No federal digital services tax (DST) exists as of 2023. The U.S. has resisted international DST frameworks (e.g., OECD Pillar 1) and instead focuses on trade agreements to prevent foreign DSTs from targeting U.S. tech companies. Proposals for a federal DST have been discussed in Congress but lack traction (e.g., past bills like the Digital Services Tax Act of 2021).
- **Effective Date/Status**: No active legislation as of 2023; potential for 2025-2026 proposals given global pressure.
- **Impact on Invoica**: No immediate federal DST impact, but future legislation could target platforms facilitating digital transactions, potentially imposing new taxes on Invoica’s services.
- **Source**: Congress.gov (search for "digital services tax"); U.S. Treasury statements on OECD negotiations.
