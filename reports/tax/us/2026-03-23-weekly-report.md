### 2. Digital Services Tax (DST) at Federal and State Levels
- **Jurisdiction**: Federal
- **What Changed/Proposed**: As of April 2023, there is no federal digital services tax in the U.S. The U.S. Trade Representative has opposed international DSTs (e.g., in France) and threatened retaliatory tariffs but has not proposed a domestic equivalent. Discussions in Congress (e.g., Senate Finance Committee hearings in 2022) suggest potential future interest in taxing digital advertising or services, but no legislation is enacted.
- **Effective Date/Status**: Not enacted; speculative for 2025-2026.
- **Impact on Invoica**: A federal DST, if introduced, could apply to platforms facilitating digital transactions, potentially increasing operating costs through new tax liabilities.
